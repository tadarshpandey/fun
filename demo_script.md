# The Brutal Tech Lead: Hackathon Demo Script

Follow this script exactly on stage to guarantee the "Wow Factor" and get judges laughing.

## ⚠️ Prerequisites (Before you go on stage)
1. Ensure your laptop volume is UP.
2. Ensure you are using **Google Chrome** (it has the best built-in Web Speech API).
3. Ensure you have given the site Microphone permissions.
4. Have a dummy PDF file ready on your desktop (name it `Resume.pdf`).

---

## 🎤 The Pitch (Storyboarding)

**You:** "Every AI interview tool on the market is too polite. They say 'Good try! But maybe improve here...' That's not how the real world works. To actually improve, you need to feel the heat. So, we built 'The Brutal Tech Lead'. It doesn't sugarcoat. It simulates the most stressful, judging senior developer you'll ever meet."

*(Turn to the screen)*

**You:** "Let me show you. I'm going to upload my resume."
*(Drag and drop `Resume.pdf` into the dropzone).*

---

## 🤖 The Hook
*(The app immediately starts pulsating "PROCESSING DATA")*

*(The computer speaks out loud):* 
> "Oh... I see you highlighted 'React Developer'. Let's see if you actually know what happens under the hood or if you just copy-paste from StackOverflow."

**You (to judges):** "Ouch. Alright, let's see what it's got."

*(The computer continues out loud):*
> "Question 1. What is the Virtual DOM in React?"

---

## 🎭 The Failure Path (Displaying the Smart Logic)
**You (to judges):** "I'm going to act like a typical nervous applicant to show you how fast the Speech-to-Text and Analysis works with zero latency."

*(You click and hold the "HOLD TO SPEAK" button. As you speak, the words immediately appear in the Live Transcript box).*

**You (Acting nervous):** "Um... well, the virtual dom is... uh... basically like a faster dom... and um... you know, react uses it."

*(Release the button. The app flashes "Analyzing your nonsense...". Instantly, the Roast appears.)*

*(The computer reads the roast out loud):*
> "You used filler words 4 times. That's not confidence, that's confusion. Fix your articulation."

**You (to judges):** "Notice how it didn't just give generic feedback. It specifically detected my filler words, counted them, and roasted me for my lack of confidence."

---

## 🏆 The Closing
*(Click 'Next Question')*

**You:** "Behind the scenes, we aren't wasting time and money calling an LLM for every single syllable. We built a lightning-fast, rule-based inference engine that analyzes speech patterns, cadence, and semantic keywords instantly. It's cost-effective, zero-latency, and most importantly... it's memorable. Thank you!"

*(Drop mic)*
